Reflexive polyhedra

This form allows the retrieval of weight systems defining five-dimensional reflexive polyhedra with given Hodge numbers and Euler characteristic. Three of the numbers fix the others by the following relations:

chi = 48 + 6 (h1,1 − h1,2 + h1,3)
h2,2 = 44 + 4 h1,1 + 4 h1,2 − 2 h1,3
