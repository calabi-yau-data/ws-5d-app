Reflexive Polyhedra

Weight systems defining five-dimensional reflexive polyhedra. You can search by Hodge numbers and Euler characteristic: enter one, two, or three numbers! Three of the numbers fix the others by the following relations:

chi = 48 + 6 (h1,1 − h1,2 + h1,3)
h2,2 = 44 + 4 h1,1 − 2 h1,2 + 4 h1,3

After entering the numbers press "Retrieve". The subsequent list of properties and weight systems will update. A full list of weight systems and a list of possible properties will be accessible if the number of matching weight systems is small enough.

Non-reflexive Polyhedra

Weight systems defining five-dimensional non-reflexive polyhedra. You can search by vertex count nv, facet count nf, and point count np: enter one, two, or all three numbers!

After entering the numbers press "Retrieve". The subsequent list of properties and weight systems will update. A full list of weight systems and a list of possible properties will be accessible if the number of matching weight systems is small enough.
